Eco-Friendly Packaging Redesign

Project Overview

This project involved a complete redesign of the packaging for a line of sustainable consumer products. The goal was to create packaging that not only reflected the brand's commitment to environmental responsibility but also enhanced the user experience and reduced material waste.

Challenges & Solutions

Key challenges included sourcing sustainable materials that met our quality standards and designing a structure that was both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Our solution involved using recycled cardboard and biodegradable inks, along with a modular design that minimized excess packaging. The final product was a significant improvement in both sustainability and user satisfaction.

Tools & Technologies

This project utilized a range of design tools and technologies, including Adobe Illustrator for graphic design, CAD software for structural design, and prototyping tools for physical mockups. We also collaborated closely with material science experts to evaluate and select the most appropriate sustainable materials.
